Who We Are

A student-led team building impact through service

The For Good executive board brings together students from diverse backgrounds to lead projects, organize events, and build meaningful community partnerships.

Together, they guide the organization’s mission of creating consistent, tangible impact in Charlottesville.

What the exec board leads

Service projects, outreach, partnerships, operations, marketing, and finance.
