About Us

Doing good in our own backyard.

The For Good Project at UVA is a student-led organization focused on hands-on service, youth education, and building meaningful impact in the Charlottesville community.

See Our Work Meet the Team

Who We Are

Hands-on, collaborative, and community-driven.

The For Good Project is a group of UVA students committed to making tangible impact in Charlottesville. We focus on direct service — from food distribution to youth outreach — while building strong relationships with local organizations.

We believe impact comes from showing up consistently and working directly with the community.

What We Do

Three pillars of impact.

Service Projects

Food drives, meal pass-outs, and hands-on service events that directly support the Charlottesville community.

Youth Education

We visit schools and youth programs to teach service, leadership, and community engagement.

Community Partnerships

We collaborate with local organizations and UVA groups to expand reach and long-term impact.

Our Impact

What we've done so far.

From donating over 200 lbs of food to the Blue Ridge Area Food Bank, to running monthly meal distributions and raising funds through student-led events, our work is focused on consistent, measurable impact.
