Our Mission

Driving change through hands-on, collaborative service.

"We design and implement projects that make a tangible impact — addressing Charlottesville's most pressing and evolving needs."

Who We Are

🤝

🏘️

💚
Care & compassion
At our core, we're building a healthier, more compassionate Charlottesville — one project at a time.

What We Do

Three pillars of impact.

01

Service Projects

From meal pass-outs to food drives and bake sales, we run hands-on projects that directly benefit Charlottesville residents — including the unhoused community and the Blue Ridge Area Food Bank.

02

Youth Education

We go into local schools to teach kids about service and civic engagement — encouraging care, compassion, and the idea that anyone can make a difference in their community.

03

Community Partnerships

We collaborate with CIOs like Food Assist and events like Hug a Hoo, building lasting relationships that let us do more together than we ever could alone.

Featured · Food Drive

BRAFB Food Drive with Food Assist

In collaboration with Food Assist, we ran a week-long food drive delivering directly to the Blue Ridge Area Food Bank. We worked closely with BRAFB staff, established a lasting partnership, and collected over 200 lbs of food — all in one week. This is a partnership we're excited to continue growing.

200+ lbs of food collected
1 wk collection window

Partner: Food Assist CIO
Beneficiary: Blue Ridge Area Food Bank

Fundraising

Bake Sales for BRAFB

All proceeds from our bake sales are donated directly to the Blue Ridge Area Food Bank. In a single day, we raised over $400 — proof that small acts of generosity add up fast.

$400+ raised in one day

Ongoing · Monthly

Meal Pass-Outs

Once a month, a group of 6–8 volunteers assembles bagged meals from club donations and heads to the Charlottesville Downtown Mall to hand them out to unhoused community members. We show up with compassion, reduce stigma around homelessness, and make real human connections.

~25 meals per pass-out
Monthly recurring service

Competition · Cav Food Pantry

Superbowl of Giving

Through the Cav Food Pantry's annual Superbowl of Giving drive, The For Good Project was among the highest contributing CIOs at UVA — demonstrating our members' commitment and the strength of our community.
