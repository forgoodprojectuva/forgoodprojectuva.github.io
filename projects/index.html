Our projects focus on direct service, partnerships, and meaningful impact in Charlottesville.

Our Work

What we do

Food Drives

We organize campus-wide food drives to support organizations like the Blue Ridge Area Food Bank, mobilizing students to donate essential items and raise awareness about food insecurity.

Meal Pass Outs

Our meal distribution initiatives provide direct support to individuals in need while building meaningful connections within the Charlottesville community.

Fundraising & Outreach

Through bake sales and campus events, we raise funds and awareness for local causes, expanding our impact through collaboration.
