Who We Are

A student-led team building tech for good

The For Good executive board brings together students from engineering, computer science, public policy, and design to build impactful projects.

Together, they guide development, manage operations, and grow the organization’s mission of using technology for social good.

What the exec board leads

Project development, outreach, partnerships, operations, marketing, and finance.
