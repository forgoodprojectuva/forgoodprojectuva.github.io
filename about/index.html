Our mission

Mission

Who we are

The For Good Project at UVA is a student-led organization dedicated to driving positive change in the Charlottesville community by addressing its most pressing and evolving needs. Through hands-on service, creative initiatives, and strong partnerships with local businesses, organizations, and fellow UVA CIOs, we design and implement projects that make a tangible impact. At its core, our mission is to drive change through hands-on and collaborative service.

A unique part of our work includes visiting local elementary and middle schools to teach students about our main projects, and how they too can get involved. By engaging directly with local residents and institutions, we aim to inspire service-minded leadership, strengthen community connections, and create a lasting culture of care and compassion.

As we grow, and the Charlottesville community’s needs change, our club will take on a new “main focus” that will address Charlottesville’s most pressing needs, all while remaining rooted in our goal of building a healthier, more compassionate Charlottesville.

Our Approach

Three pillars of impact

Service Projects

Meal pass-outs to the unhoused, food drives, bake sales, tabling fundraisers, and community volunteering — all focused on direct, hands-on impact.

Youth Education

We engage directly with Charlottesville’s youth, visiting schools to teach service, leadership, and how students can make a difference in their communities.

Community Partnerships

We collaborate with local organizations, businesses, and UVA CIOs to expand our reach and build sustainable, long-term impact.
