FISH at UVA is a student-led organization focused on frugal, sustainable healthcare innovation through interdisciplinary collaboration, hands-on design, and community-rooted problem solving.

Explore Projects

Our Mission

Designing practical healthcare solutions with sustainability, accessibility, and real-world use in mind.

We partner with physicians, researchers, and healthcare professionals to turn real-world problems into practical, cost-effective solutions.

Our team brings together students from engineering, public health, data science, and the College of Arts and Sciences to approach healthcare challenges from multiple perspectives.

Interdisciplinary

15+ majors and minors

FISH brings together students with different academic backgrounds to build stronger, more thoughtful solutions.

Community-rooted

Real challenges

Projects begin with problems faced by patients, practitioners, and partners in the healthcare community.

Hands-on

Design to prototype

Members move beyond ideas into research, prototyping, testing, and iterative improvement.

What Drives Us

A shared approach to innovation.

Patient-centered

We focus on problems that matter to real people and design with usability, context, and impact in mind.

Frugal & sustainable

We aim to create maximum value with minimal resources, building solutions that are practical, scalable, and durable.

Interdisciplinary

The best ideas come from different perspectives. We combine engineering, medicine, public health, and more to solve problems holistically.

How It Works

From challenge to solution.

We start by understanding real pain points in healthcare settings, then move through collaborative research, brainstorming, prototyping, and testing. Throughout the process, we work closely with physicians, faculty, and community partners to make sure the final result is not only innovative, but useful and sustainable in practice.

01

Share the challenge

We begin with a real problem or unmet need in healthcare.

02

Research and define

We learn from users, partners, and context before shaping a direction.

03

Prototype and test

Ideas become tangible through design work, feedback, and iteration.

04

Refine collaboratively

We keep improving with input from the people the solution is meant to serve.
