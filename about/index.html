About FISH at UVA

Frugal innovation for more sustainable healthcare.

FISH at UVA — Frugal Innovations in Sustainable Healthcare — is a student-led organization at the University of Virginia building thoughtful, accessible, and human-centered healthcare solutions through interdisciplinary collaboration.

Get Involved Meet the Team

Who We Are

Student-led, interdisciplinary, and impact-driven.

Frugal Innovations in Sustainable Healthcare is a student-led organization that brings together students from engineering, medicine, business, public policy, design, and other disciplines to work on meaningful healthcare challenges.

We believe the best ideas come from collaboration across backgrounds and from designing with real constraints in mind. Our goal is to create a space where students can learn, build, and contribute to more accessible and sustainable healthcare innovation.

Student-led

Built by students who take initiative in design, prototyping, and outreach.

Interdisciplinary

Different backgrounds working together around the same healthcare problems.

Collaborative

A community where students learn from one another and build together.

Purposeful

Focused on solutions that can make a real difference beyond the prototype stage.

Our Approach

Frugality, sustainability, and real-world impact guide everything we build.

We believe innovation should be practical, resource-aware, and grounded in the realities of healthcare systems and the people they serve.

That means designing with constraints in mind, thinking beyond short-term prototypes, and prioritizing solutions that are implementable, adaptable, and meaningful.

Frugality

We focus on thoughtful solutions that do more with less and avoid unnecessary complexity.

Sustainability

We consider long-term viability, responsible design choices, and systems that can endure.

Real-world impact

We aim to respond to actual needs with ideas that can improve access, experiences, and outcomes.

Our Story / Timeline

How FISH started and where it’s headed.

The beginning

A shared idea

FISH started with the belief that healthcare innovation should be more accessible, collaborative, and grounded in real-world constraints.

The present

A growing student community

Today, FISH supports hands-on projects, events, and partnerships that help students learn by doing and collaborate across disciplines.

The future

Deeper impact

We are continuing to expand our reach through stronger project pipelines, broader collaboration, and more opportunities for students to build with purpose.

The Founders

The students who helped start FISH.

FISH was founded this year by students who saw an opportunity to create a space for interdisciplinary, sustainable, and impact-driven healthcare innovation at UVA.

What began as an idea has quickly grown into a community centered on collaboration, creativity, and building solutions that respond to real healthcare challenges. As a new organization, our founders helped shape not only our mission, but also the kind of community we hope FISH will continue to become.
