Potential Competitions

Opportunities we hope to prepare for

These are some of the competitions and design challenges we hope to submit to as our internal projects continue to grow. They represent opportunities for teams to share their work, receive feedback, and challenge themselves to build stronger, more polished solutions.

Preparing for these submissions often means developing clear slide decks, documenting the design process, validating the problem and solution, and strengthening prototypes through testing and iteration.

Know of another opportunity?
