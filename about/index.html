About The For Good Project

Doing good in our own backyard.

The For Good Project at UVA is a student-led organization dedicated to driving positive change in the Charlottesville community through hands-on service, youth education, and strong local partnerships.

See Our Work Meet the Team

Who We Are

Hands-on, collaborative, and community-driven.

The For Good Project is a group of students passionate about making a tangible difference in the Charlottesville community. We focus on direct service — from food drives to meal pass-outs — while building meaningful relationships with local organizations and people.

Our goal is simple: create consistent, compassionate impact through projects that respond to real community needs.

Hands-on

We prioritize direct action — organizing drives, distributing meals, and showing up.

Collaborative

We partner with local organizations and other UVA groups to expand our impact.

Community-focused

Everything we do is centered on Charlottesville’s needs.

Impact-driven

We focus on tangible results, not just ideas.
